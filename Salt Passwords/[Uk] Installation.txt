How to apply the Salt Passwords MOD on OxyGen PreMODded Board ?
---------------------------------------------------------------

Having installed OxyGen PreMODded Board, Upload in the root of this one the password.php file.
Then execute the file (ex.: http://www.your_website_name.com/board_folder/password.php).

If the message which follows appears, It is because the Salt Passwords MOD is henceforth active.

Successfully installed.
You may now delete

The get rid of the file of your FTP, disconnect from the board and re-login.
